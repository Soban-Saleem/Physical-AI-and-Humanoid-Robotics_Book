# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook project. This textbook aims to teach full-system integration skills combining ROS 2, simulation environments, and applied AI for embodied systems.

## About

This textbook is designed for graduate students, professional engineers, and advanced hobbyists transitioning to robotics. It covers:

- ROS 2 (The Robotic Nervous System)
- Simulation environments (The Digital Twin)
- Applied AI for embodied systems (The AI-Robot Brain)
- Vision-Language-Action models (VLA)
